LASAGNA

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian pasta dish made by layering flat pasta sheets with rich meat sauce, creamy cheese (typically ricotta, mozzarella, and parmesan), and often béchamel sauce, then baking until bubbly and golden. It's a hearty, comfort food favorite known for its satisfying layers of flavor and its ability to feed a crowd.

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion
  • Canned Tomatoes
  • herbs
  • sugar
  • spices & seasonings
  • noodles
  • cheese
  • eggs

Steps

  1. Cook 1 lb ground beef with diced onion and garlic, drain fat
  2. Add pasta sauce to meat mixture and simmer
  3. Cook lasagna noodles according to package directions, drain
  4. Mix ricotta cheese with egg and Italian seasoning
  5. Layer in baking dish: sauce, noodles, ricotta mixture, mozzarella cheese
  6. Repeat layers, top with parmesan cheese
  7. Cover with foil, bake at 375°F for 25 minutes
  8. Remove foil, bake 25 more minutes until bubbly
  9. Let rest 10 minutes before serving
